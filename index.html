Who Are We?

A couple-owned business born from their passion and love for cats and coffee. We have proudly operated on bustling St. Charles Historic Main Street for five years and counting.

Customers can enjoy delicious coffee and hot food while hanging with the store owners' five cuddly, adorable cats!

This is no ordinary cat café. We offer essential cat supplies for your own furball. If you’re thinking about welcoming a cat into your home, we partner with Petfinder to help connect you with rescued cats in need of a loving family.
